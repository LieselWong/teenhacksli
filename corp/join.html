Get Involved

Help make TeenHacks LI possible




Students

Join the TeenHacks LI organizing team! Be a part of making this 300+ person event possible and join a team of highly motivated students who are passionate about STEM.

We are completely student-ran, so we rely on people like you to make TeenHacks LI possible.

We are NOT actively recruiting at this time.

Please check back in June 2020 for our next round of recruiting.

Companies

Interested in partnering with TeenHacks LI? We would love to have you be a part of our event in some way. We offer marketing, recruitment, and on-site activation opportunities. Reach out to our team at team@teenhacksli.com to talk more.

TeenHacks LI remains committed to being a free event to all, however, running a 24-hour event for over 300 students costs money. We are always looking for sponsors who can help support our event. All monetary donations are tax deductible (EIN: 81-2908499). We will also work with you to see how TeenHacks LI can best support your company’s mission.
