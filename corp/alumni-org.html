Organizers' Alumni Database

Welcome to the TeenHacks LI Alumni Database! This site, as a part of our Alumni Relations program, serves to compile a list of past organizers of TeenHacks LI, stretching all the way back to our first event in Fall 2018. All information was submitted voluntarily and our alumni are always happy to hear from you! TeenHacks LI is still a part of you, even after the event, and our alumni go into a variety of fields. Feel free to take advantage of this resource and reach out about literally anything - internships, college, career paths, life, etc.
