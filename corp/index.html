TeenHacks LI

Hack for Our Future

Mission

TeenHacks LI is a student run non-profit with a mission to build a collaborative hacker community here on Long Island, with an emphasis placed on innovation and entrepreneurship. We run two 24-hour high school hackathons every year, gathering hundreds of the brightest minds across the tri-state area and beyond. We pride ourselves in creating a unique computer science education experience you can’t find in the classroom.

Community

Students who come to TeenHacks LI join a diverse community of student programmers from all across the country. Meeting peers from different schools, socioeconomic backgrounds, and experiences, participants make lifelong friends that share a passion for creating. The event itself is full of engaging activities dedicated to fostering this community. After the event, students also join an alumni network full of opportunities and conversations to stay connected.

To be as welcoming and inclusive of a community as possible, our event is completely free and our outreach targets a large demographic area, including communities that are historically underrepresented in the tech field.

Education

TeenHacks LI is a learning experience. This comes through many avenues: self-exploration, workshops, and mentorship. We partner with tech companies and other non-profits to offer resources and technologies for students to innovate with. Each event is jam-packed with workshops hosted by professional developers, teaching students how to use cutting edge technologies. Throughout the event, mentors roam the creation space to provide more individualized help for projects. Students not only learn hard skills, but also how to work on a team, the process of project development, and finally how to pitch their end product.

Students learn skills that prepare them for a career in tech, ones that can not be learned in a classroom.

Innovation

Free from the constraints of the classroom, students at TeenHacks LI have the freedom to create whatever they can imagine. Providing the resources and guidance to explore new technologies, TeenHacks LI is the perfect venue to create projects students would normally never have the chance to. From hardware to AI, students find creative solutions to real world problems by thinking outside the box.

These are the skills that create a new generation of creative thinkers.

Testimonies

"History shows us that great innovation and discovery can happen at any age. Now more than ever, our future depends on the next generation’s ability to collaborate to harness creativity in order to solve some of the most intractable challenges we face. TeenHacks LI is vital because they promote breakthroughs in scientific innovation but also inspire our young people to pursue careers in STEM."

Laura Curran, Nassau County Executive
