About TeenHacks LI

TeenHacks LI is Long Island’s premier and only active 24-hour hackathon, founded by students, for students. A hackathon is an event where innovative students come together to engage in “collaborative coding,” creating a project from beginning to end. On September 29-30, high school students from across the tri-state area will get together to learn, create, and share at The Coder School Syosset. We will have professional mentors and an array of workshops. Our goal is to provide a space unlike any other for students to build whatever they want, share what they make, and compete for prizes. We hope to build a community of student programmers here on Long Island, with an emphasis placed on innovation and entrepreneurship. In addition, we believe that computer science education should be accessible to all, so the event is completely free and we will provide four meals.
