TeenHacks Li is only 3 days away!!

We're super excited for the event and wanted to pass along some important logistical information so pleaes read this email throughly.

When: September 29th-30th, 3pm to 3pm. 
Check-in opens at 2:30pm on Sept. 29. Please arrive by 2:55pm!
Plan to depare at 3pm on Sunday.
*Note: if you plan on arriving late or leaving early, please let us know in advance!

Address: The Coder School Syosset, 45 Ira Road, Syosset, NY 11791

Transportation:
By train:The venue is located adjacent to the Syosset LIRR station on the Port Jefferson branch. We will have greeters on the platform for the east-bound trains arriving at 2:24pm and 2:51pm on Saturday.
By car: there is a big parking lot infront of the venue. You can be dropped off or park if driving self.

Waiver [IMPORTANT]: You must complete the waiver found here. Please sign, save as a PDF named LastnameFirstname, and email to team@teenhacksli.com. If you do not have this completed, you will not be allowed to enter the venue space.

What to bring: Your laptop, phone, chargers, sleeping bag, and hygine products (ex. toothbrush, deoderant, etc.). Also, anything you may need for your project (ex. hardware). Also, you MUST bring your student ID. 

Hacker resources: You can check out some of the resources we’ll have at http://teenhacksli.com/#hackerres. We have over $1,500 worth of hardware and even more software.  

Slack: Please join our Slack channel for important announcements day-of and to communicate with others. 

Project submissions: We will be using HackerEarth for project submissions and judging (similar to Devpost). You can find our event at https://teenhacksli.hackerearth.com/. You can register and create a team now, or at the event. Project submissions will be due at 11:59am on Sunday.
I know for a lot of students this is their first hackathon, so here is some more information for first-timers:

The Goal: The goal is to create an entire project, from beginning to end all in the span of the 24-hours with what you learned. The project can be anything, as serious or silly as you want. It doesn't have to be perfect, just create something cool and meaningful. At the end, each team will present their creations to a panel of judges and compete for prizes.

24-Hours: Yes, this event is 24-hours. No, sleep is not mandatory. However, it won't be 24-hours of straight coding. We have fun activities planned throughout the night. If you do want some shut-eye though, bring a sleeping bag and feel free to sleep anywhere in the venue. We will have quite hours as the next day is a school day, so you should be able to get a few hours of sleep.

Beginners: We have a series of workshops and mentors lined up to help both beginners and advanced coders. If you don't know how to code, don't worry. Just have an open-mind and a willingness to learn. We even have a beginner's category prize for you!

Food: We’ll provide 3 full meals: Saturday dinner, Sunday breakfast and lunch. There will also be snacks and drinks throughout the event. If you have any dietary restrictions we can not guarantee meal options so please plan accordingly (but we'll try out best).

Cost: This event is completely free, thanks to our generous sponsors! 
If you have any questions, please feel free to email us at team@teenhacksli.com or text/call us at (516) 584-3252. 
 
We are excited to see you at TeenHacks LI!
 
Best regards,
